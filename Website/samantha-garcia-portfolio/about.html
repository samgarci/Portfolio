About Me

Creating has always been a big passion of mine. Whether it is writing songs, drawing a graphic novel, making websites, or the other dozens of creative projects I have going on. I am a classically trained singer-- you could say music was the first new language I ever learned. I believe it gave me a knack for learning rules, putting different elements together, and seeing how they fit. Although I have this background in music and create art, web design is something new I have been learning. Through my studies at Reynolds Community College (and some extra online classes in my free time), I have realized what a great convergence this avenue is with my interests. I can create visually pleasing elements that can go into websites I design. I get to problem-solve and see a project go from sketch to result, just like with a piece of art or a song! Just like the other mediums I use to create, web design is exciting and lights a fire in me to always learn and improve my craft.
